Paris, France


Paris, often called the "City of Light", is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world. Known for its romantic atmosphere, historic landmarks, and world-class cuisine, Paris attracts millions of tourists every year.

Top 5 Attractions in Paris

  1. Qutub Minar – A must-see icon of Delhi,India.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – Famous for its shopping and cafes.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A romantic way to see the city.

Must-Try Foods in Paris

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery, and best enjoyed fresh.
  • Macarons – Colorful and sweet French delicacies.
  • Escargot – A unique dish of cooked snails.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes served with sweet or savory fillings.
  • French Cheese – A must-try for cheese lovers.
